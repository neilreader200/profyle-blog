# Welcome to the Profyle Blog

This is the inaugural post on our newly migrated blog system.

## Why we moved

We moved off the Framer CMS to Cloudflare Pages + Worker because Framer doesn't expose a public write API. With this new setup, our n8n Content Factory can push markdown files directly to GitHub, and they appear live within seconds.
