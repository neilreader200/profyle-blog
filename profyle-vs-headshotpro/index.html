Profyle vs HeadshotPro: Honest Comparison for UK Professionals (2026)

Last updated: 1 May 2026 · UK perspective

Both Profyle and HeadshotPro generate AI-powered professional headshots from selfies — same broad category, different execution. The question for UK professionals isn't "which is the better service" in the abstract; it's which one fits how UK customers buy, work, and use the photos.

Short answer: Profyle if you're UK-based — the GBP pricing, UK GDPR compliance, ICO registration, and 1-24 hour delivery match how UK professionals actually buy headshots. HeadshotPro if you don't care about country of operation — it's the established US-market category leader with more raw output volume.

When Profyle is the better fit

  • You're UK-based and want GBP pricing. No FX surprise on your card statement, no transaction fees, no currency conversion confusion.
  • You want UK GDPR + ICO compliance baked in. Profyle is registered with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (C1906880). For NHS staff, solicitors, and anyone in regulated industries, this matters.
  • You're in a specific UK profession. Profyle has dedicated niches for estate agents, solicitors, NHS staff, teachers, accountants, consultants, recruiters, corporate teams, and university applicants. Each gets profession-appropriate styling.
  • You want fast turnaround. Premium tier delivers in 1-2 hours.

When HeadshotPro is the better fit

  • You're not in the UK. If you're in the US, HeadshotPro's currency, support timing, and brand recognition match your context better.
  • You want maximum raw output volume. HeadshotPro's higher tiers deliver up to 200 headshots in some plans.
  • You don't need profession-specific styling. If you just need generic "professional" headshots, the niche distinction matters less.

Verdict

For a UK professional, the choice is straightforward: Profyle. The GBP pricing and ICO registration alone justify the choice for anyone in regulated industries. Profession-specific styling is the bigger differentiator for UK customers in finance, law, medicine, and education — three sectors where being "appropriately dressed for your role" outranks being "polished" generically.

For a US professional, HeadshotPro is a perfectly reasonable choice and the original category leader.
