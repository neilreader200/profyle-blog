Profyle vs Secta Labs: Honest Comparison for UK Professionals (2026)

Last updated: 1 May 2026 · UK perspective

Secta Labs positions itself at the premium end of AI headshot generation — high price point, polished output, considered a benchmark for quality. Profyle positions in the value-meets-quality middle, optimised for UK professionals who want studio-quality output without paying premium-tier prices.

Short answer: Profyle if you want studio-quality output at workable UK pricing. Secta Labs if budget isn't a constraint and you want the most polished output available.

When Profyle is the better fit

  • You want studio-quality output at £29-49 instead of £100+. Profyle's quality is genuinely competitive at a quarter the price.
  • You're getting headshots for a specific UK role. Niche styling matters; Secta Labs is style-range based, not profession-based.
  • You want fast turnaround. Premium tier in 1-2 hours, vs 1-2 days at Secta Labs.
  • You're paying with GBP. No FX losses on every transaction.

When Secta Labs is the better fit

  • Budget isn't a constraint and you want maximum output volume. 250+ headshots vs 80-160.
  • You're in the US and brand recognition matters. Secta has built name recognition in the US tech/exec audience.
  • You're willing to wait 1-2 days for the most polished result. Some professionals prioritise output quality over speed.

Verdict

For most UK professionals, Profyle delivers ~90% of Secta's quality at ~30% of the price, with profession-specific styling Secta doesn't offer. Secta makes sense if budget isn't a factor and you want the absolute best the AI headshot category produces — but for the price gap to close, you need to value the marginal quality improvement at over £75 per session.
