Profyle vs Aragon AI: UK vs US AI Headshot Comparison (2026)

Comparison guide

Last updated 30 April 2026 — refreshed weekly with current competitor pricing.

Aragon AI is one of the largest US-based AI headshot services. They've built strong volume in the American market on a "hyper-realistic, fast turnaround" pitch and decent pricing — typically $35 for the basic tier, $55 for standard, $99 for the high-volume pack.

For UK professionals, the question isn't whether Aragon is good. They are. The question is whether their US-shaped product is the right fit when you're working in a UK professional context — and whether the FX maths actually leave them cheaper than the local alternative once your bank takes its cut.

How they're different at the structural level

Most AI headshot providers do roughly the same thing: train on selfies, output styled headshots. The differences sit in what's around the core product.

Aragon's strengths are speed (their delivery is fast, often under an hour), volume (their high-tier delivers 200+ images), and a US-corporate styling default that suits American audiences well. Their weaknesses, for a UK buyer, are the same things every US-headquartered service has: USD pricing with FX margin, US data subprocessors, styling that wasn't built around UK professional conventions.

Profyle's strengths are the inverse: GBP pricing without FX, UK ICO-registered controller, and styles tuned to nine UK profession contexts. We're not faster than Aragon at headline speed (we ship Premium in 1–2 hours, not 30 minutes). We're not bigger (Aragon has more US customers and history). We're built for a different market.

Five questions UK buyers ask

"Aragon's headline price is $35. Profyle's is £29. So Aragon is cheaper, right?"

Not once you actually pay. $35 USD on a UK card converts to roughly £28 + a foreign-transaction fee of 2.75–2.99% — you'll see £28.80–£29.10 hit your statement. The two services are within a pound of each other on the entry tier. The "cheaper" framing only works on a screenshot.

Effective price difference at entry tier: negligible.

"Will my photos look UK-appropriate?"

Aragon's default styling is US-corporate — bright American office backgrounds, sharper lighting, broader smiles, often with US-specific outfit cues. The output is good but it's tuned for an American audience. If your photo is going on a UK estate agency listing, a Chambers profile, or an NHS trust staff page, those visual conventions are different.

Profyle's profession packs are tuned to UK professional contexts. Aragon's are tuned to US ones.

"Where does my biometric data sit?"

Aragon is a US company, so your selfies are processed under US privacy law with subprocessors typically in the US. UK data flows out under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework adequacy chain, which is lawful but is a longer custody trail than UK organisations in regulated sectors usually want.

Profyle is registered with the UK Information Commissioner's Office under C1906880. Reader Digital Ltd, a UK company, is the data controller. Selfies are processed under UK GDPR.

If your employer or regulator cares about UK data residency, Profyle is the lower-risk choice.

"Is the speed difference real?"

Yes. Aragon often delivers in 30–60 minutes; Profyle Premium targets 1–2 hours. If you need a headshot in the next 30 minutes, Aragon is faster. If "before end of day" is the bar, both make it comfortably.

Aragon wins on raw speed. Margin is small.

"What if I don't like the result?"

Aragon offers a refund if you're unsatisfied, with terms set by them about what counts as unsatisfied. Profyle's Professional and Premium tiers include a Likeness Guarantee: a free redo if the first pass doesn't look like you, and a full refund if the redo also misses. The redo is included in the original price.

Profyle's redo path is more explicit and lower-friction.

Where Aragon would be the right call

If you're based in the US, your audience is US-corporate, you specifically need 200+ images for high-volume use cases, and you're not bothered by UK-specific styling, Aragon is a strong product. We don't compete well on volume at our top tier (120 vs 200+), and for buyers who specifically want the largest possible gallery, Aragon's Pro plan is the bigger pack.

Speed-sensitive use cases also favour Aragon — if you're literally minutes away from a press deadline and need a headshot now, the 30-minute delivery is a meaningful edge.

Where Profyle is the right call

UK pros, UK regulated professions, anyone who'd rather pay in GBP, anyone whose target audience expects a UK professional look. The price difference once FX is included is nearly nothing. The UK-fit and data-residency arguments stay.

For estate agents specifically, the styling difference is the most visible: a Profyle Estate Agent pack is tuned to what a Rightmove headshot looks like, where Aragon's default styling produces something closer to a Zillow realtor headshot. They're both good photos. They're not the same photo.

Verdict

Aragon AI is a competent US-built product. If you're in the US, or your work doesn't have a strong UK-context dependency, they're a perfectly reasonable choice. We're not interested in pretending otherwise.

For UK professionals, Profyle is built for you specifically. The pricing's in your currency. The data sits with a UK-regulated controller. The styling is tuned to nine UK professional niches. The Likeness Guarantee gives you a free redo. None of those things are dramatic on their own — but they add up to a product that fits a UK buyer in a way an American product, however good, can't quite.
