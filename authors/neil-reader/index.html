About

Neil is the founder and sole operator of Reader Digital Ltd, the UK company behind Profyle. He started Profyle after spending several years working with small UK firms — solicitors, accountants, NHS clinicians, and estate agents — and noticing how often a perfectly good professional was held back by an out-of-date or simply weak headshot. The pattern was the same every time: studio photography was too expensive and too time-consuming for a quick refresh, but a phone selfie wasn't quite good enough either. Profyle is the answer to that gap, built specifically for the UK market.

Neil writes about Profyle's product decisions, the broader AI headshot industry, and the patterns he sees across the UK professional services sectors Profyle serves. His posts are practical rather than promotional — he'd rather a reader leave with something genuinely useful even if they don't end up buying.

What Neil writes about

  • Decisions about UK GDPR, ICO compliance, and biometric data handling — the boring-but-important infrastructure of running a service like this in the UK
